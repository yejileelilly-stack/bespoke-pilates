FAQ

Common questions

Do I need prior Pilates experience?
Not at all. The Trial Session is designed for complete beginners. We start with a body assessment and build at your own pace.
What should I wear and bring?
Comfortable, form-fitting workout clothes. Grip socks are recommended — I have some available. Bring water and arrive 5 minutes early.
Where is the studio?
A quiet home studio 3 minutes from Katong Park MRT. The exact address is shared after booking confirmation.
Is there parking available?
Yes, street parking is available nearby. Katong Park MRT is the easiest option — just a 3-minute walk.
What is the cancellation policy?
Please contact Yeji directly for any cancellations or changes. Late cancellations (under 24 hours) or no-shows are non-refundable.
Can I do sessions while pregnant?
Yes — prenatal Pilates is one of my specialisms. Sessions are adapted for every trimester. Please mention your stage when booking.
How do I pay?
Payment is via PayNow. A QR code is shown at the end of booking. Your slot is confirmed once payment is received and Yeji will SMS you to confirm.
Do 10-session packs expire?
10-session packs are valid for 6 months from purchase. They are non-transferable and non-refundable once purchased.
